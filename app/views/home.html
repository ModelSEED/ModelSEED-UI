What is the ModelSEED?


ModelSEED is a resource for the reconstruction, exploration, comparison, and analysis of metabolic models.

Fast

Faster reconstruction and gapfilling of models from annotated genomes

Easy

Editable media, models, and more makes experimenting with models a breeze

Microbes and Plants

Unified resources for microbial and plant modeling, allowing for cross-disciplinary research

Enabling Science

Incorporating cutting-edge and experimental methods/algorithms

Open Source

Open source with community contributions

Programatic Access

API clients for Perl and Python are currently available here.
